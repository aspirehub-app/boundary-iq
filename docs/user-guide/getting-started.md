# Getting Started - 5 minutes to your first field

This guide gets you from "never opened it" to "tracking in the field." No
account, no setup, no cost.

## Before you start

You need:

- A smartphone (Android or iPhone) or a computer with a browser.
- Location/GPS turned **on**.
- For the field itself: a clear view of the sky for the best GPS signal.

> **Tip:** For the best experience in the field, open BoundaryIQ from an
> **HTTPS web address** (see [Installing on your phone](#install-on-your-phone))
> and add it to your home screen. GPS in a browser only works over HTTPS,
> `localhost` or by opening the file directly on a computer.

## Step 1 - Open the app

Open BoundaryIQ in your browser. You'll see a **map** and a **control panel**
(on the side on a computer, at the bottom on a phone). The panel has four tabs:

| Tab | What it's for |
|---|---|
| **Field** | Draw or enter your field boundary |
| **Equipment** | Enter your equipment width |
| **Track** | Start live tracking |
| **Layers** | Map background, cadastre overlay, backup |

## Step 2 - See your land clearly (optional but recommended)

Open the **Layers** tab → set **Background** to **Satellite imagery (Esri)**.
Now you can see actual fields, tracks edges from above.

To overlay official parcel boundaries (Serbia): tick **Show cadastral parcels**,
keep the **GeoSrbija / RGZ** preset, press **Apply overlay**, then zoom to your
parcel.

## Step 3 - Add your field

Open the **Field** tab. The fastest way:

1. Press **＋** to create a new field and give it a name (e.g. *North plot, Site A*).
2. Press **Start drawing**.
3. **Tap each corner** of your field on the map, going around the edge.
4. Press **Finish**.

You'll see your field outlined in green, with its **area** and **perimeter**
shown automatically.

> Prefer to drive the boundary instead? Use **Start walking** and drop a GPS
> point at each corner as you drive around. Or paste `lat, lng` lines under
> **Enter coordinates**. See the [User Manual](user-manual.md) for all methods.

## Step 4 - Tell it your equipment width

Open the **Equipment** tab:

1. Set **Implement / working width** to the widest part of your equipment (e.g.
   `3` for a 3-metre plough).
2. Set a **safety margin** (e.g. `0.5` m) to absorb GPS wobble.
3. Press **Apply**.

A **yellow safe-zone** now appears just inside your field. Stay inside it and
your implement never crosses the border.

## Step 5 - Start tracking

Open the **Track** tab → press **▶ Start tracking**. Allow location access when
your browser asks.

A big banner appears at the top:

- 🟢 **Inside field** - you're safe, with room shown.
- 🟡 **Approaching border** - slow down (you'll hear a beep).
- ⛔ **Crossing / outside** - steer back (loud alarm + vibration).

That's it - you're protecting your boundary.

## Try it at your desk first (Test mode)

Want to understand the alerts without driving? On the **Track** tab press
**Enable test mode**, press **Start tracking**, then **tap around the map** to
move a simulated equipment in and out of your field. Watch the banner change
colour and listen to the alerts.

## Install on your phone

If you opened BoundaryIQ from a web address (HTTPS):

- **Android (Chrome):** menu **⋮ → Add to Home screen**.
- **iPhone (Safari):** **Share → Add to Home Screen**.

It now opens full-screen like a normal app and works offline.
